ME218C is the third quarter of a three quarter series Graduate Class on Mechatronics at Stanford University. This year, students were asked to create a remote controlled boat that could be controlled in a unique way in order to play a game of "King of the Atolls", a capture the flag game involving 2 teams of 2 boats. We invite you to explore our design on how we created our boat and controller.
