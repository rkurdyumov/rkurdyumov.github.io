Meet the Team

Team Money Talks (from left to right): Logan Ames, Kyle Nekimken, Ruslan Kurdyumov

Logan Ames
I attended Kettering University near Detroit, Michigan for my undergraduate. I am from Beaverton near Portland, Oregon and enjoy all the outdoor activities that the Pacific NW has to offer. I am an avid snowboarder and have actually worked as a snowboard instructor at Mt. Hood Meadows. I enjoy mountain climbing and rockclimbing. One of my more recent adventures involved rappelling beside a 100 foot waterfall.

Kyle Nekimken
I grew up in Los Alamos, New Mexico where I spent most of my free time playing sports, playing my trombone, hiking in the summer and skiing in the winter. I attended Cal Poly-SLO for undergrad and received a BS in ME. At Cal Poly I continued exploring the outdoors with snowshoeing, rock climbing, and backpacking whenever possible. Some of my other interests include cooking, cycling, and playing disc golf.

Ruslan Kurdyumov
I was born in Ukraine and lived in Kiev until I was 6. My family moved to the US in 1994 and we have lived in Sacramento since then. My parents forced me to attend Russian school for 8 years, but I'm grateful for it now. I'm a huge Sacramento Kings fan, and I hope to attend a few games since they're 2 hours away. I went to Caltech for undergrad and double majored in ME and Business. It's my first time living in the Bay Area, so I'm excited to see how it compares to Sacramento and SoCal.
