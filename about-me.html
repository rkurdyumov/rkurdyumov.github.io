About Me

I studied Mechanical Engineering & Business at Caltech, where I had the chance to work on robotic arm control at JPL, a Mars rover protoype, and lots of other cool projects.

At Stanford, I finished my MSME, focusing on Mechatronics and Controls.  I worked on a few awesome robots, including a basketball player and a bike-controlled catamaran.  I also got to work on the LIGO project.  They famously announced the first direct gravity wave detection on February 11, 2016!

I'm interested in robotics and mechatronics. and have the most experience in those fields, but I'm open to other cool projects as well.  Check out my work and send me an email if you'd like to get in touch.
